## üìè Image Specifications

### Recommended Image Properties:
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Size**: 300x200 pixels (3:2 aspect ratio)
- **Quality**: High resolution, clear and sharp
- **Background**: White or neutral background preferred
- **Style**: Consistent lighting and angle across all images

### Image Content Guidelines:
- Show the actual pulse/legume product clearly
- Use good lighting to show natural colors
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds
- Ensure the product fills most of the frame
- Use consistent styling across all images

## üîÑ Fallback System

The website includes a fallback system:
- If an image is missing, it will automatically show a placeholder with the product name
- Placeholder format: Green background with white text showing the product name
- This ensures the website works even if some images are missing
